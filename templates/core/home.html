About

Engineer who builds things that work.

I am Adebowale Abidemi Aminat — a Software Engineer and AI Systems Developer with experience building backend systems, AI-powered applications, and workflow automation that help businesses operate more efficiently.

My final-year project at ABUAD — a USSD transaction monitoring system for mobile banking — became a co-authored research paper now under international review. I build systems that solve real operational problems, not demos.

I hold a B.Sc. in Computing from Afe Babalola University, where I also led technical skill-building programmes through NACOS SkillHub.

Research & Innovation

Published work.

Transaction Tracking Agent: A USSD-Based Payment Observability and Recovery Middleware

Adebowale Abidemi Aminat & Prof. Opani Meshack Aweh — Afe Babalola University

A middleware system for real-time USSD transaction state tracking and automated recovery in mobile banking. Introduces a six-stage state machine, a three-phase recovery engine (Retry → Reversal → Escalation), and a formal distinction between HUNG and FAILED transactions — addressing a critical observability gap in sub-Saharan African fintech infrastructure.

JournalInternational Journal of Electronic Banking (IJEBank)
PublisherInderscience Publishers
StatusUnder review
